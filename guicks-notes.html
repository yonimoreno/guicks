Free Online Notepad — Auto-Saves in Your Browser

A clean, distraction-free notepad that lives entirely in your browser. Write notes, organise them with tags, and search through them instantly. Everything saves automatically — no account, no cloud, no data leaving your device.

Private by design

Notes are stored in your browser's localStorage — they never touch a server. This means your notes are completely private, work offline, and are always available even without an internet connection. Clearing your browser data will erase them, so export anything important.

Organise with tags

Add tags to any note to group related content together. Filter your notes by tag to find what you need quickly. Tags are great for separating work notes, personal ideas, shopping lists and anything else you want to keep organised.

When to use a browser notepad

Perfect for quick thoughts you need to jot down fast, temporary scratch pads while working at a computer, copying text from one place to another, or drafting messages before sending them. For anything long-term, consider exporting to a document.
