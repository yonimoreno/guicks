How to get the best translation

For the most accurate results, translate shorter passages rather than entire documents. Simple, clear sentences translate more reliably than complex idioms or slang. If a translation seems off, try rephrasing the source text in simpler language. Machine translation is excellent for understanding meaning but may not always capture nuance perfectly.
